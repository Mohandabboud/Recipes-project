Prep: 10mins

  Cook: 50mins

  Total: 10mins

  Servings: 4

  Yield: 4 servings





Nutrition info

Ingredients

1: 2 table spoon olive oil

2: 2 table spoon chooped onion

3: ¼ cup green peas

4: 1 bay leaf

5: 1½ cups dry rice

6: 3 cups water

7: salt to taste

Directions

Step1

In large sauepan over a medium-low heat, warm the oil. Add onion and saute for 3 to 5 minutes in green peas, bay leaf, and jasmine rice. Stir to coat the rice

Step 2

Pour 3 cups water into the saucepan add the salt. Increase the heat to medium and let the rice come to aquick simmer. Reduce heat to low and let rice simmer lightly and sit uncovered until all the liquid is absorbed. Cover the rice and remove from heat. Let sit for about 40 minutes.
